Capabilities

Chempure has an extensive track record in the design, supply and construction of plants worldwide for the treatment of water, wastewater treatment solutions, sewage water treatment solutions and sludge treatment solutions. In addition to being well versed in a full range of established and conventional processes, the company offers leading edge advanced water treatment solutions, technologies. Moreover, clients will continue to be provided with up-to-date solutions to their water and wastewater treatment problems through the company's programme of constant process technology development and search for suitable technologies from overseas associates.
